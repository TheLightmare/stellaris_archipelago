# Stellaris

## Where is the options page?

The [player options page for this game](../player-options) contains all the options
you need to configure and export a config file.

## What is randomized?

Stellaris integrates with Archipelago through a hybrid milestone/tech system:

- **Milestones** fire automatically as you play (surveying systems, researching techs, colonizing planets, building fleet power)
- **AP Techs** appear in your research pool as researchable technologies with the Archipelago logo, named after the items they send to other players

## What do I need to receive?

Your empire receives progressive unlocks from other players:

- **Progressive Ship Class** - Destroyers, Cruisers, Battleships, Titans, Juggernaut
- **Progressive Weapons/Defenses** - Weapon and shield/armor tiers
- **Progressive FTL** - Hyperdrive upgrades and Jump Drives
- **Progressive Starbase** - Starbase tier upgrades
- Plus unique items, resource caches, and traps

## What is the goal?

The default goal is **Victory** (survive to year 2400). Other goals include defeating the endgame crisis, forming the galactic imperium, completing an ascension path, or checking all locations.

## EnergyLink

Stellaris supports EnergyLink at a 1:1 exchange rate with Factorio. Use the EnergyLink edicts to deposit or withdraw energy credits from the shared multiworld pool.
